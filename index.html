Calculus 1 Guide

Derivative Rules

Derivative of a Constant

\( \frac{d}{dx}c = 0 \)

\( \left(c\right)^{'} = 0 \)

The Constant Multiple Rule

\( \frac{d}{dx}cf(x) = c\left(\frac{d}{dx}f(x)\right) \)

\( \left(cf(x)\right)^{'} = cf^{'}(x) \)

The Power Rule

\( \frac{d}{dx}\left(x^n\right) = nx^{n-1} \)

\( \frac{d}{dx}\left(ax^n\right) = nax^{n-1} \)

The Sum and Difference Rules

\( \frac{d}{dx}\left(f(x) \pm g(x)\right) = \frac{d}{dx}f(x) \pm \frac{d}{dx}g(x) \)

\( (f(x) \pm g(x))^{'} = f^{'}(x) \pm g^{'}(x) \)

The Product Rule

\( \frac{d}{dx}\left(f(x)g(x)\right) = \left(\frac{d}{dx}f(x)\right)g(x) + \left(\frac{d}{dx}g(x)\right)f(x) \)

\( (f(x)g(x))^{'} = f^{'}(x)g(x) + g^{'}(x)f(x) \)

The Quotient Rule

\( \frac{d}{dx}\left(\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}\right) = \frac{g(x)\left(\frac{d}{dx}f(x)\right) - f(x)\left(\frac{d}{dx}g(x)\right)}{g(x)^2} \)

\( \left(\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}\right)^{'} = \frac{g(x)f^{'}(x)-f(x)g^{'}(x)}{g(x)^2} \)

The Chain Rule

\( \frac{d}{dx}f(g(x)) = \left(\frac{d}{dx}g(x)\right)\left(\frac{d}{dx}f(u)\right) \space where \space u = f(x) \)

\( (f(g(x)))^{'} = g^{'}(x)f^{'}(g(x)) \)

Trigonometric Derivatives

Basic & Reciprocal Functions

\( \frac{d}{dx}\sin(x) = \cos(x) \)

\( \frac{d}{dx}\cos(x) = -\sin(x) \)

\( \frac{d}{dx}\tan(x) = \sec^{2}(x) \)

\( \frac{d}{dx}\csc(x) = -\csc(x)\cot(x) \)

\( \frac{d}{dx}\sec(x) = \sec(x)\tan(x) \)

\( \frac{d}{dx}\cot(x) = -\csc^{2}(x) \)

Inverse Functions

\( \frac{d}{dx}\arcsin(x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} \)

\( \frac{d}{dx}\arccos(x) = \frac{-1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} \)

\( \frac{d}{dx}\arctan(x) = \frac{1}{1-x^2} \)

\( \require{physics} \frac{d}{dx}\arccsc(x) = \frac{-1}{\abs{x}\sqrt{1-x^2}} \)

\( \frac{d}{dx}\arcsec(x) = \frac{1}{\abs{x}\sqrt{1-x^2}} \)

\( \frac{d}{dx}\arccot(x) = \frac{-1}{1-x^2} \)

Hyperbolic Functions

\( \frac{d}{dx}\sinh(x) = \cosh(x) \)

\( \frac{d}{dx}\cosh(x) = \sinh(x) \)

\( \frac{d}{dx}\tanh(x) = \sech^{2}(x) \)

\( \frac{d}{dx}\csch(x) = -\csch(x)\coth(x) \)

\( \frac{d}{dx}\sech(x) = -\sech(x)\tanh(x) \)

\( \frac{d}{dx}\coth(x) = -\csch^{2}(x) \)

Logarithmic and Exponential Derivatives

Logarithmic Functions
Exponential Functions

\( \frac{d}{dx}\ln(x) = \frac{1}{x} \)

\( \frac{d}{dx}\log_{a}(x) = \frac{1}{x\ln(a)} \)

\( \frac{d}{dx}e^{x} = e^{x}\)

\( \frac{d}{dx}a^{x} = a^{x}\log(a) \)

Curve Sketching

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Function Analysis
    1. Find \((x,y)\) for all \(x-intercept(s)\) and the \(y-intercept\)
    2. Determine the \(Domain\) and \(Range\)
    3. Use Limits or Divide by Zero Error to Check for Vertical and Horizontal Asymptotes
    4. Take Limits to Positive and Negative Infinity to Determine End Behavior
  2. First Derivative Test
    1. Take the First Derivative
    2. Find all Critical Points, or Extrema, by Solving for \(x\) where \(f^{'}(x)=0\)
    3. Check for Increasing or Decreasing Intervals Above, Below, and Between each Extrema
      1. \( f^{'}(x\pm\delta) \lt 0 \rightarrow \) Decreasing
      2. \( f^{'}(x\pm\delta) \gt 0 \rightarrow \) Increasing
    4. This test is due to the fact that first derivatives only change signs at the extrema
      1. \(+ \rightarrow - \) changes imply a Maxima
      2. \(- \rightarrow + \) changes imply a Minima
  3. Second Derivative Test
    1. Take the Second Derivative
    2. Find all possible Points of Inflection by solving for \( x \) where \( f^{''}(x) = 0 \)
    3. Check for Concavity Above, Below and Between Points of Inflection
      1. If \(f^{''}(x \pm \delta ) \lt 0 \rightarrow \) Concave Down
      2. If \(f^{''}(x \pm \delta ) \gt 0 \rightarrow \) Concave Up
